Update 3.8.2 is live with major balance adjustments, a new boss encounter, and expanded language support.

## Highlights

- Added **Mysterious Chamber Event** — a new end-run boss encounter available from Floor 20.
- **Phoenix** damage reduced for better balance.
- **Enemy Crit Damage** scaling reduced at high Curse levels (up to 47% less at Curse 10).
- **Floor Bosses** are now +1 level stronger.

## 8 New Languages

Arabic, French, Hindi, Indonesian, Italian, Korean, Polish, and Turkish are now supported!

## Smaller improvements and fixes

- Adjusted **Monarch** stats (ATK ×2 → ×1.8, CritDmg ×1.3 → ×1.2).
- Improved auto mode: Boss Doors setting now warns about attacking the Dungeon Monarch.
- **Hardcore** warning message is now translatable.

Thank you for all the feedback and ideas — they helped shape this update.
